Our Beginnings

In 2018, WHO has labelled India as the world’s ‘most depressing country’. Moreover, between 1990 to 2017, one in seven people from India have suffered from mental illness ranging from depression, anxiety to severe conditions such as schizophrenia, according to a study. It is no exaggeration to suggest that the country is under a mental health epidemic.

Our Vision

Nirvata endeavours to Improve the community’s understanding of mental health and the mind-body connection between mental and physical health. Our vision is ambitious, A society which actively values and supports people on their journey to positive mental health.

Our vision is to provide culturally competent, holistic, and wellness focused services that promote children's social-emotional development, prevent development of mental health challenges, and address social-emotional problems that currently exist.

Our mission is to create and promote mental health communities by spreading awareness to reduce the stigma , providing affordable mental health services across India .
